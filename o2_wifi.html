O2 Wi-Fi Re-designed

Big Data is a key catalyst to modern digital innovation. However, our biggest challenge is not technical execution, its human perception and adoption.

I reported directly to the head of O2 Client Experience and identified O2â€™s problem with data monetization through a substantial body of generative research. This entailed, utilising innovative research techniques with members of the public to identify their mental model of data monetization, their feelings towards it and their direct/indirect experiences with it. This research was analysed thematically to uncover insights that lead an iterative design process with multiple iterations and tests with users. The final design uses skeuomorphic micro interactions to represent the users data in the digital space, communicating to the user the cost of sharing their data alongside the reward.
