Conversational Tooling Design

My evaluation of the design problem and re-design of the product resulted in 70% more data collected form users in a fraction of the time.

THE PROBLEM

The Smart Skills team wanted to improve the efficiency of their product. The Smarter Skills tool allows managers to better allocate recourses based on their teams skill set. However no one in the team was rating their skills. Through heuristic evaluation and evaluative user research techniques I uncovered user pain that the Engineering team had not previously considered or were refusing to acknowledge. Most notably that, users were “loosing the will to live” and describing the activity as “hell”, “slow, tedious and pointless”, the emotional response to the tool was extremely negative.

THE APPROACH

This in-depth understanding of the user guided ideation and the iterative design process lead to a solution that subtly leveraged Gamification principals. Tested prototypes of the design showed dramatic improvements in the users emotional response and the time it took them to complete.

THE SOLOUTION

Previously users were taking 40-50 minutes with 30% of users not completing the task, now it took them 5 minutes with 100% completion.
